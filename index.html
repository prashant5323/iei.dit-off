About Us

The Institution of Engineers (India) [IEI] is the largest multi-disciplinary professional body of engineers, established in 1920 with its Headquarters located in Kolkata and incorporated under Royal Charter on 9th September, 1935 by the then His Majesty of King George V. The Royal Charter endowed the Institution with the responsibility to promote the general advancement of engineering amongst its members and persons attached to the Institution. he Institution of Engineers (India) is the first professional body to represent India in several International Bodies, such as the World Mining Congress (WMC), the World Federation of Engineering Organizations (WFEO), the Commonwealth Engineersâ€™ Council (CEC), the Federation International du Beton (fib), and the Federation of Engineering Institutions of South and Central Asia (FEISCA). Pune International Airport Visit

The document summarizes a one-day visit by second year B.E. students in Electronics and Communication Engineering to Pune International Airport in lohegoan Pune, India. The students received presentations on the airport's departments and operations. They then visited the Air Traffic Control tower and Equipment Room, where staff explained air traffic management. Students observed live air traffic monitoring and were excited to learn technical details. The visit provided career insights and was a great learning experience for the undergraduate students.
